# REFERENCE — fldentalassisting.com Brand Audit

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Primary navy | `#012D50` / `#023C6A` | Headings, nav, section labels |
| Teal/cyan gradient | `#9EFCFF → #40CDD0` | Primary "Apply Now" CTA button |
| Teal hover | `#48C3C6` / `#72E6E9` | Interactive states |
| Pale aqua wash | `#F4FBFB` | Section background bands |
| Dark gray | `#4F4F4F` | Subheads, body labels |

Navy + bright teal is the signature pairing — professional + energetic.

## Typography

- **Display:** Oswald (condensed, uppercase, letter-spacing -1 to -2px, weights 300–700), sized 75–95px in hero
- **Body:** Inter (400/500/700)
- **Sub-nav:** Roboto 500
- Also loads Adobe Typekit kit `rtl7jue.css`

## Logo & Imagery

- Logo file: `logo.svg`, alt text currently "fida" (a11y/SEO improvement: use "Florida Institute of Dental Assisting")

- Single testimonial: Dr. Hall (Amelia Perfect Smile)

- CTAs: "Apply Now" + "Schedule a Tour" repeated 3x
- Phone: 904-674-3131 (click-to-call in header)
- Zoho SalesIQ chat site-wide
- Gravity Forms newsletter capture with TCPA/SMS opt-in

## Accreditation & Trust

- Facebook + Instagram in footer
- External citations to ADA.org and fdaausa.org
- **Gap:** no CODA, COE, FDOE, or Florida Board of Dentistry badges on homepage

## Voice Summary

Aspirational-practical, second-person, career-change friendly. Leans on affordability, speed, and local Jacksonville identity rather than credentials.

## Recommended Fixes

1. Add accreditation/compliance badges (or at minimum text citations) to homepage
2. Fix logo alt text from "fida" to full school name
3. Populate empty `alt` attributes across hero and section images
4. Add more than one testimonial
5. Consider replacing Adobe Stock hero video with real FIDA classroom footage

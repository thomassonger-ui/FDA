# REFERENCE — Florida Department of Education

## Scope

FDOE oversees vocational/career education in Florida, including post-secondary workforce programs. Where FIDA offers a program that falls under FDOE jurisdiction (e.g., the 6-month Dental Assisting entry-level program), FDOE's framework applies.

## Authoritative sources

- Florida Department of Education: https://www.fldoe.org/
- Commission for Independent Education (CIE) if licensed under CIE: https://www.fldoe.org/policy/cie/

## Typical areas of interest

- Program length and clock-hour reporting
- Student catalog requirements and enrollment agreements
- Instructor qualifications documentation
- Placement reporting for career programs
- Financial disclosures
- Record retention

# REFERENCE — Assessment Weights

Gradebook weighting for the Radiography for Dental Personnel course.

## Weighting Model

Overall grade is composed as follows (percentages sum to 100%):

| Component | Weight |
|---|---|
| Module quizzes (Topics 01–15, equally weighted) | 50% |
| Final competency assessment (Topic 16) | 40% |
| Practice/H5P activity completion | 10% |

## Module Quiz Mechanics

- Each quiz: 10–15 questions, randomized from a pool
- Passing score per module: **80%**
- Attempts: unlimited, highest score counts
- Each module quiz = 50%/15 ≈ **3.33%** of final grade

## Final Competency Mechanics

- Comprehensive, covers all 16 topics
- Passing score: **80%**
- Attempts: 3 maximum; contact instructor for a 4th
- Weighted at **40%** of final grade

## Practice/H5P Completion

- Binary — student either completed the required practice activities or not
- Counts as **10%** of final grade
- Designed to reward active engagement, not just test-passing

## Course Passing Criteria

A student passes the course when:
- Overall weighted grade ≥ **80%**, AND
- Final competency assessment ≥ **80%** (non-negotiable), AND
- All 16 modules marked complete

## Why these weights

- Quizzes at 50% keep continuous accountability across topics
- Final at 40% ensures integrated mastery
- Practice at 10% encourages engagement without making it gameable

Any change to this model requires updating:
- Moodle gradebook
- `REFERENCE-completion-rules.md` in `/moodle`
- Course syllabus

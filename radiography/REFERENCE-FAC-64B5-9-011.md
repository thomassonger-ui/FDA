# REFERENCE — Florida Administrative Code 64B5-9.011

## What it is

Section 64B5-9.011 of the Florida Administrative Code sets training requirements for dental personnel (other than dentists and licensed dental hygienists) who expose and process dental radiographs in Florida.

## Who it applies to

- Dental assistants (registered or otherwise) who take X-rays
- Any non-licensed staff operating dental radiographic equipment under a dentist's supervision

**Does not apply to:** Florida-licensed dentists and licensed dental hygienists (separate scope).

## What the rule requires

A person covered by the rule must complete a training program that covers:

1. Radiation physics and biology
2. Radiation safety and protection (for operator and patient)
3. Dental X-ray equipment (operation, maintenance, quality control)
4. Image production — film and digital
5. Infection control specific to radiography
6. Intraoral and extraoral techniques
7. Anatomy, image evaluation, and basic pathology recognition
8. Legal and ethical considerations

The training must be verifiable — a certificate of completion is typically produced.

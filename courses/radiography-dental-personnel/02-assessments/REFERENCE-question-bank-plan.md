## Item authoring checklist

- [ ] Stem is a complete question or sentence
- [ ] Exactly one correct answer (no "all of the above" / "none of the above" unless necessary)
- [ ] Distractors are plausible — not obviously wrong
- [ ] No clinical-scenario items beyond assistant scope (recall: "assistants identify; the dentist diagnoses")
- [ ] Reading level is accessible (target 8th–10th grade where regulation allows)
- [ ] Item maps to at least one stated learning objective in its module
- [ ] Item flagged with correct sub-topic category for random draw

## SME review

Every item must be reviewed by a Subject Matter Expert (typically the instructor of record or a dentist consultant) before going live. Track reviews in the Moodle item bank "review status" tag.

## Item security

- Disable "show feedback during quiz" for summative quizzes and the final exam.
- Final exam: require Safe Exam Browser; no feedback shown until after close.
- Do not expose the bank in downloadable or print form; export for audit only.

## Where items go in Moodle

- One **Question Category** per module (e.g., "RAD-M1-XRayTube")
- Sub-categories per item topic (e.g., "RAD-M1/kVp-mA", "RAD-M1/filtration")
- Random-draw settings in module quiz pull from the sub-categories proportionally

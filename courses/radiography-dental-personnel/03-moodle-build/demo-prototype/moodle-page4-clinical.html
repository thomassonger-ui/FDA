Tubehead setup & safety check

Before your Clinical Day, you'll perform this competency in-person. This page shows what the evaluation looks like and what you're being scored on.

!
Regulatory framing

This skill must score ≥ 3/4 ("Supervised") on the Florida Board of Dentistry competency rubric. Any score below 3 triggers a 30-day remediation re-test on this competency only.

Procedure steps

1

Confirm prescription

Read the prescription back to the supervising dentist. Confirm the series and any patient-specific modifications before touching equipment.

2

Place barriers

Cover the tubehead, control panel, and sensor. Use barrier tape on any surface that will contact a contaminated glove.

3

Verify machine settings

Set kVp, mA, and exposure time per prescription. Read the settings back aloud — this is a double-check your supervising dentist can hear.

4

Seat and shield the patient

Lead apron first, thyroid collar second. Confirm the collar is flat and at the chin line.

5

Position and expose

Operator positions the tubehead. Step behind the shielded wall or recommended distance. Verify the patient is still and expose.

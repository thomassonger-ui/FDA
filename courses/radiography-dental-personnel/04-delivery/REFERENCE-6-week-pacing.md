## Tips to finish on time

- Block two weeknights (≥ 1.5h each) + one weekend session for quizzes and H5P.
- Do required reading before the video — the video reinforces, it doesn't replace.
- Attend live check-ins even if you're caught up; they help stick the material.
- Save Clinical Day questions for Monday's live.

## If you fall behind

- **Week 1–2 behind:** catch up within 7 days; use the next week's check-in to flag blockers.
- **Week 3–4 behind:** contact instructor within 48 hours. You may be pushed to the next cohort's Clinical Day.
- **Failing a module quiz twice:** the module re-locks; request a reset from the instructor.
- **Failing the final exam:** 1 retake after 7-day cooling period + targeted review assignment.
- **Failing the clinical rubric:** 30 days to re-test on deficient competencies only.

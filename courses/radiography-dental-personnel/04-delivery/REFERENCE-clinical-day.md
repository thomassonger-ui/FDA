# REFERENCE — Clinical Evaluation Day

## What it is

A single 8-hour in-person day where students demonstrate the 7 clinical competencies live. Occurs in Week 6 of the cohort. Passing the clinical rubric is non-negotiable for certification.

## Student requirements

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Attire | Full scrubs, closed-toe shoes |
| ID | Government-issued photo ID |
| Arrival | 15 minutes early |
| Bring | Printed student acknowledgment form; any PPE the program specifies for the cohort |

## Day schedule (0800–1700)

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 0800 | Check-in + attendance (mod_attendance) + barrier-sequence checklist review |
| 0830 | Station rotation begins — 7 stations |
| 1200 | Lunch (provided or on own, per cohort) |
| 1245 | Resume rotations |
| 1600 | Final sign-off + remediation scheduling (if needed) |
| 1700 | Dismiss |

## The 7 stations

Each station corresponds to one rubric competency:

1. PPE selection + donning
2. FMX paralleling technique
3. Bitewing series
4. Panoramic imaging
5. Image evaluation
6. Infection control during radiography
7. Radiation safety (operator + patient)

Students rotate through all 7; instructor scores each on the 4-point rubric (see [`/02-assessments/REFERENCE-clinical-rubric.md`](../02-assessments/REFERENCE-clinical-rubric.md)).

## Instructor / supervising dentist

- Florida-licensed dentist supervises the day per FAC 64B5-9.011 scope.
- Instructor of Record present throughout.
- Co-sign on rubric and remediation log.

## Site requirements (client/Contractor coordination)

- Clinical site with ≥ 1 operatory suitable for intra-oral radiography and ≥ 1 panoramic unit
- Standard PPE stock
- De-identified training images available for landmark ID stations (≥ 100 intra-oral, ≥ 50 panoramic)
- Dentist supervisor booked; Moodle shows site address, map, directions

## What triggers remediation

- Any competency scored below 3 triggers remediation:
  - 30 calendar days to re-test
  - Re-test scoped to the deficient competency(ies) only
  - New rubric signed; replaces the original for that competency
- No certificate issued until all competencies ≥ 3

## Backup / reschedule

- Weather or instructor unavailability triggers a rescheduled Clinical Day (same cohort or next cohort's slot).
- Student emergency (documented): may defer to next cohort's Clinical Day; enrollment extended within 90-day window.

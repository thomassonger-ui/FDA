# REFERENCE — Module Specs (M1–M12)

Build-ready specifications for each content module. Every module follows the same 7-activity template (see [`TEMPLATE-module.md`](./TEMPLATE-module.md)). This file captures the specifics that vary per module.

All modules: 80% passing quiz, 2 attempts, randomized draw from 25–30 item bank, videos require ≥ 90% watched. H5P interactives require ≥ 80% unless noted.

---

## M1 — X-Ray Tube & Beam

- **Hours:** 5 · **FDOE:** 17.02, 17.01 · **Prereq:** M0
- **Item bank:** 32 items · **Quiz draw:** 12 items
- **Scope:** Tubehead anatomy, kVp/mA, bremsstrahlung, characteristic radiation, thermionic emission, inherent vs. added filtration, collimation.

## M2 — Radiation Physics & Biological Effects

- **Hours:** 5 · **FDOE:** 17.01 · **Prereq:** M1
- **Item bank:** 28 items
- **Scope:** Stochastic vs. deterministic effects; units (rem, rad, sievert, gray); FMX effective dose; tissue weighting factors.

## M3 — Radiation Protection

- **Hours:** 5 · **FDOE:** 17.03 · **Prereq:** M2
- **Item bank:** 30 items · **Quiz draw:** 15 items
- **Scope:** ALARA principle, MPD (Maximum Permissible Dose), lead apron + thyroid collar rules, dosimeter interpretation.
- **Special activity:** SCORM module on dose reduction.

## M4 — Infection Control in Radiography

- **Hours:** 4 · **FDOE:** 17.12 · **Prereq:** M3
- **Item bank:** 28 items · **Quiz draw:** 12 items
- **Scope:** CDC Standard Precautions, barrier placement, operatory sequencing, sensor/plate disinfection.

## M5 — Selection of Techniques (ADA/FDA)

- **Hours:** 4 · **FDOE:** 17.06 · **Prereq:** M4
- **Item bank:** 26 items · **Quiz draw:** 12 items
- **Scope:** FDA/ADA Selection Criteria; modifications for pediatric, adult, edentulous, pregnancy, gag reflex cases.

## M6 — Paralleling & Bisecting-the-Angle

- **Hours:** 6 · **FDOE:** 17.06 · **Prereq:** M5
- Build detail lives in [`/00-overview/REFERENCE-blueprint.md`](../00-overview/REFERENCE-blueprint.md) as the exemplar "Sample Module Detail".
- **Key activities:** holder selection simulator (H5P), 18-image FMX walkthrough video.

## M7 — Film Processing / Mounting / Duplication

- **Hours:** 4 · **FDOE:** 17.04, 17.05, 17.09, 17.10 · **Prereq:** M6
- **Item bank:** 26 items · **Quiz draw:** 12 items
- **Scope:** Developer / fixer / rinse / dry chemistry; labial-dot mounting convention; film duplication; film inventory practices.

## M8 — Image Evaluation

- **Hours:** 4 · **FDOE:** 17.08, 17.11 · **Prereq:** M7
- **Item bank:** 30 items · **Quiz draw:** 15 items
- **Scope:** Image quality metrics (density, contrast, sharpness, magnification, distortion); intra-oral and panoramic landmarks.
- **Scope boundary:** "Assistants identify landmarks; the dentist diagnoses." This language must appear verbatim in the module.

## M9 — Quality Control: Errors

- **Hours:** 4 · **FDOE:** 17.11, 17.06, 17.07 · **Prereq:** M8
- **Item bank:** 25 items · **Quiz draw:** 15 items
- **Scope:** Error atlas — cone-cut, overlap, elongation, foreshortening, reversed film, reticulation, fogging; QC schedule.

## M10 — Extra-Oral / Panoramic

- **Hours:** 5 · **FDOE:** 17.07 · **Prereq:** M9
- **Item bank:** 28 items · **Quiz draw:** 15 items
- **Scope:** Focal trough; Frankfort plane; midsagittal plane alignment; panoramic patient preparation.

## M11 — Panoramic Errors

- **Hours:** 4 · **FDOE:** 17.07, 17.11 · **Prereq:** M10
- **Item bank:** 26 items · **Quiz draw:** 15 items
- **Scope:** "Eight classic panoramic positioning errors" — chin tipped up, chin tipped down, slumped posture, twisted, forward/back positioning, tongue not against palate, lips open, patient movement. Ghost/double image artifacts.

## M12 — Florida Law / Ethics / HIPAA

- **Hours:** 3 · **FDOE:** Standard 03 + 17.12 · **Prereq:** M11
- **Item bank:** 29 items · **Quiz draw:** 15 items
- **Scope:** FAC 64B5-9.011, §466.017(5), F.S., scope of practice, HIPAA application scenarios.
